My Little Cthulhu

Thursday, January 1, 2009

 

Cthulhu is the fictional giant creature that embodies all evil and chaos in H. P. Lovecraft's sci-fi novels.  It’s described as a huge squid-like creature that invokes absolute terror.


Cthulhu is well known in geek circles - and even plays a main figure in the UserFriendly.org comic strip.  So, when John Kovalic designed this hilariously sick and twisted toy (see right) as a parody to “My Little Pony”, I had to get it.  Plus, its Boxing week, so it was on sale :-)


It comes with two terrified victims (shown right) and the warning on the bottom of the box says:


WARNING - CHOKING HAZARD

Not for children under the age of 14.
