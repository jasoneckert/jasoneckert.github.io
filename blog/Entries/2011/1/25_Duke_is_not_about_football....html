Duke is not about football...

Tuesday, January 25, 2011

 

Most people (myself included) use clothing to express our interests or make a statement.  Today I wore my Duke University t-shirt. 


For the third time since the beginning of last summer, some stranger commented on it along the lines of "A Duke fan eh? They play good football."


Football?  Duke University is the home of USENET - that is why I have the damn shirt.  Duke first introduced a USENET server in 1979 and they had it running until 2010 (over 30 years!).   That is definitely worth getting a t-shirt over in my mind.  Football?  Meh.....
