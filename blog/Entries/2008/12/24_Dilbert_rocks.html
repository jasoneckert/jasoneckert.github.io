Dilbert rocks

Wednesday, December 24, 2008

 

I have always been a Dilbert fan - to me it was a geekier version of the Far Side.  So, when I got a copy of Dilbert 2.0: 20 Years of Dilbert (a huge freaking book in its own case), I read the whole thing quite fast (with the help of Red Bull).  The book is great, and the cartoonist adds his commentary to many of the cartoons, which is hilarious because Scott Adams is one twisted individual.
