IBM hard drive from 1981

Tuesday, May 27, 2008

 

The picture above is an IBM Model 3380 Hard Drive from 1981.  It weighs approx. 70 pounds and barely fits into the trunk of my car.  The maximum capacity of this hard drive is 2.52GB and was the first hard drive to breach the 1GB barrier for a price tag of $140,000US.  The best part is that it looks like a nuclear device or jet engine.  There are nine disks that rotate in the center of the hard disk - you can see them in the picture below:
