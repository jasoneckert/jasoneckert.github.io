Installing a Ms.Pac-man multigame save kit

Sunday, March 18, 2018

 

If you’ve read other parts of my site, you already know that I collect vintage arcade games. 


Back in January, I traded some stuff for some arcade parts, and one of the things I got was a brand new Pac-man/Ms.Pac-man Multigame High Score Save Kit from HighScoreSaves.com (https://www.highscoresaves.com/pacman-ms-pacman-multigame-high-score-save-kit.html).  My daughter came over for dinner that same day, and since she thought it was neat, we decided to install it in my Ms. Pac-man cocktail table and see if it worked.  It did.....and it was awesome!


The kit itself consists of a bunch of replacement chips on small circuit boards, as well as a replacement daughter board (the small circuit board that contains the Z80 CPU on a Ms. Pac-man game).  And each of these circuit board and daughter board replacements are interconnected via a series of ribbon cables:




My daughter and I pulled the original circuit board out of the game and made sure we documented the original components just in case it didn’t work and we had to revert back to them.  Next, we replaced all the necessary components, added the ribbon cables and stuck it back in the Ms. Pac-man cocktail table:




As soon as we powered it on it worked perfectly!  It took us a little while to figure out that you had to hold down the 1-player start button for a few seconds in order to get to the game choice screen though:




Now, I can play far more than Ms.Pac-man on this old arcade machine, including the fast versions, and it will save my high scores in a high score table (original Pac-man/Ms.Pac-man games never had a high score table).  Plus, there are a ton of settings you can tweak for each game, including the ability to start at level 255 so you can see the infamous “kill screen”, or even fix the infamous kill screen bug to allow you to go past level 255 too! 


Since we installed this kit, I’ve played this arcade machine more than I’ve ever played it in the past - I absolutely love the Plus and Fast versions of each game, as well as the ability to permanently save top scores. So, if you have an original Pac-man or Ms.Pac-man arcade machine, I highly recommend installing this kit to make it even more fun than it was before!
