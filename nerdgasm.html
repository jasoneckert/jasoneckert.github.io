Get Your Geek On!

Nerds and geeks are amongst the most educated, tenacious, light-hearted and creative people on the planet. In addition to being technology-savvy, they are typically knowledgeable of world issues, as well as advocate free speech and information sharing. Nearly all nerds/geeks are hackers, but not in the malicious sense of the word (you can thank the media for that stereotype). Hackers originated in computer science in the 1950s and are people who enjoy exploring the details of programmable systems and how to stretch their capabilities. Hackers push science and technology to the next level. The word cracker refers to someone who uses computers for malicious activities. Don’t confuse the two terms - hacker, nerd and geek are synonymous, but not related to the word cracker.
